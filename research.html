My primary research interests are numerical analysis, more precisely geometric numerical integration, and stochastic modelling. A common theme is the use of tools and methods from combinatorics and algebra. More details can be found in the papers linked to below. A good starting point is perhaps the survey paper "On algebraic structures of numerical integration on vector spaces and manifolds".

My collaborators include Hans Munthe-Kaas, Kurusch Ebrahimi-Fard, Dominique Manchon, Pierre Del Moral, Simon Malham and Anke Wiese.
