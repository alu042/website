My primary research interests are related to algebraic and combinatorial aspects of geometric numerical integration and stochastic modelling. Some key tools and concepts: the combinatorics of trees and partitions, pre-Lie and post-Lie algebras, Hopf algebras, Lie group integrators, Lie—Butcher series, and flows on homogeneous manifolds.

More details can be found in the papers linked to below. A good starting point is perhaps the survey paper On algebraic structures of numerical integration on vector spaces and manifolds.

Collaborators include Hans Munthe-Kaas, Kurusch Ebrahimi-Fard, Dominique Manchon, Pierre Del Moral, Simon Malham and Anke Wiese.
